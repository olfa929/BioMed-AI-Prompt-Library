# Research Collaboration Proposal
**Category:** Academic & Research Outreach
**Task Description:** Proposing a partnership between your engineering skills and another researcher's domain (e.g., Medicine or Biology).

## 🤖 AI Prompt Template
> "Draft a collaboration proposal to [NAME], a researcher in [FIELD]. I am a Biomedical Engineer specializing in [SOFT TECH SKILLS] and [HARD TECH SKILLS]. I noticed your work in [THEIR AREA] and believe my expertise in [YOUR STRENGTH, e.g., 3D printing custom lab-ware or AI-driven diagnostics] could accelerate your data analysis. Propose a brief 15-minute call to discuss a potential pilot project."

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
- Focus on what *you* can give (the "Value Add") rather than what you want from them.

**Estimated Time Savings:** 45 Minutes
