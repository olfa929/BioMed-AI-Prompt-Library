# The "Grant Significance" Assistant
**Category:** Academic & Research Outreach
**Task Description:** Drafting the "Impact" or "Significance" section for a project funding or scholarship application.

## 🤖 AI Prompt Template
> "Write a 'Statement of Significance' for my project: [PROJECT TITLE/DESCRIPTION]. Focus on how this project addresses a gap in the current Biomedical field, specifically regarding [PROBLEM, e.g., accessibility of prosthetics]. Use persuasive, professional language. Highlight the scalability of the solution and its potential impact on patient outcomes. Target length: 300 words."

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
- Provide the AI with your "rough notes" on why the project matters to get a polished result.
