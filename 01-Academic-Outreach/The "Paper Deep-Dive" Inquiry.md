# The "Deep-Dive" Methodology Inquiry
**Category:** Academic & Research Outreach
**Task Description:** Asking a lead author for clarification on a specific technical part of their research paper.

## 🤖 AI Prompt Template
> "I am a Biomedical Engineering student reading your paper titled '[PAPER TITLE]'. I have a specific question regarding the [METHODOLOGY SECTION, e.g., feature extraction process]. Could you clarify how you handled [SPECIFIC TECHNICAL DETAIL]? I am attempting to implement a similar logic in my own research on [YOUR PROJECT]. Thank you for your contribution to the field."

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
- Mention a specific page or figure number from the paper to prove you've studied it.

**Estimated Time Savings:** 30 Minutes
