# Research Internship Cold Email
**Category:** Academic & Research Outreach
**Task Description:** Drafting high-impact, professional emails to professors for international research positions.

## 🤖 AI Prompt Template
> "Act as a Career Consultant for STEM students. Write a concise cold email to [PROFESSOR NAME] at [UNIVERSITY/LAB]. I am a Biomedical Engineering student with a C1 English level (IELTS 7). I am specifically interested in their work on [RESEARCH TOPIC/PAPER TITLE]. Highlight my experience in [KEY SKILL, e.g., SVM Modeling in R] and ask about internship opportunities for [TIMEFRAME]. Keep it under 200 words and maintain a respectful, academic tone."

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
1. Replace `[PROFESSOR NAME]` and `[UNIVERSITY/LAB]` with the recipient's details.
2. Be specific in `[RESEARCH TOPIC]` to show you've read their work.

**Estimated Time Savings:** 40 Minutes
