# Research Internship Cold Email
**Category:** Academic & Research Outreach
**Task Description:** Drafting high-impact, professional emails to professors for international research positions.

## 🤖 AI Prompt Template
> "Act as a Career Consultant for STEM students. Write a concise cold email to [PROFESSOR NAME] at [UNIVERSITY/LAB]. I am a Biomedical Engineering student with a C1 English level (IELTS 7). I am specifically interested in their work on [RESEARCH TOPIC/PAPER TITLE]. Highlight my experience in [KEY SKILL, e.g., SVM Modeling in R] and ask about internship opportunities for [TIMEFRAME]. Keep it under 200 words and maintain a respectful, academic tone."

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
1. Replace `[PROFESSOR NAME]` and `[UNIVERSITY/LAB]` with the recipient's details.
2. Be specific in `[RESEARCH TOPIC]` to show you've read their work.

## 🛠 Input
Professor Hans Katzberg, neuromuscular research group at the Krembil Brain Institute. 
Topic: Prospective Evaluation of Shimmer-Based Near-Fiber EMG Waveform Analysis for the Diagnosis of Myasthenia Gravis.
My skill: Advanced Signal Analysis

## 🛠 Output
Dear Dr. Katzberg,

I am a Biomedical Engineering student writing to express my strong interest in the neuromuscular research being conducted at the Krembil Brain Institute. I have been following your group’s work closely, particularly the prospective evaluation of Shimmer-based near-fiber EMG waveform analysis for diagnosing Myasthenia Gravis.

Your integration of wearable sensor technology with refined electrophysiological analysis aligns perfectly with my academic focus. I have developed a robust foundation in Advanced Signal Analysis, specifically in processing complex physiological waveforms to extract diagnostic features. Combined with my C1 English proficiency (IELTS 7), I am prepared to contribute effectively to a high-level clinical research environment.

I am eager to apply my technical skills to support your team’s data collection or analysis efforts. Do you have any openings for a research internship during July and August 2026?

I have attached my CV for your review and would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background in signal processing can support your current projects.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Olfa Boutiti
